Strict Health Rules

⚠️

Follow these rules strictly. Your medication works only when combined with healthy habits.

Today's compliance75%
1

Never skip a dose

Take medicine at the same time every day. Missing doses reduces effectiveness.

2

No alcohol with medication

Alcohol reacts dangerously with BP, diabetes, and antibiotic medicines.

3

Drink 8+ glasses of water daily

Helps kidneys process medicine and avoids dehydration side effects.

4

Low-sodium, low-sugar diet

Avoid maida, namkeen, pickles, fried food. Critical for BP and diabetes patients.

5

30 mins walking daily

Improves blood circulation, blood sugar control, and heart health.

6

Blood tests every 3 months

HbA1c, lipid profile, kidney function — monitor medicine effectiveness.

7

Store medicines correctly

Keep away from sunlight and moisture. Away from children.

8

Check expiry before use

Never use expired medicines. Dispose safely — don't flush down drain.
